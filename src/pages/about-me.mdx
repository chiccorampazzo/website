I am a [Career Development Fellow in Marketing and Consumer Demography](https://www.sbs.ox.ac.uk/about-us/people/francesco-rampazzo)
at the Saïd Business School, the [Leverhulme Centre for Demographic Science](https://www.demographicscience.ox.ac.uk),
and [Nuffield College](http://www.nuffield.ox.ac.uk) at the University of Oxford. I am a PhD candidate in Social Statistics and Demography at the [University of Southampton](https://www.southampton.ac.uk/demography/index.page),
during my PhD I was a Doctoral Fellow at the Max Planck Institute for Demographic Research.

I am a demographer with a broad range of research interests, which include digital and computational demography with applications in fertility, migration, and survey research.
